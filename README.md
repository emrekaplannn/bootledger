# bootledger
In today's global marketplace, product tracking looks for a standardized, secure, and
privacy-conscious approach. Most companies are looking for innovative and cost effective ways
to ensure their products are not counterfeited or lose quality due to middleman looseness. This application
project aims to address this issue by leveraging cutting-edge technology, specifically
decentralized identifiers (DIDs), embedded within QR codes. This technology allows for the
secure tracking of products and transactions through peer-to-peer systems. This way, end users
can verify whether their products are original and where they originate from, and producers can
track if any problems occur at specific nodes of the supply chain. The design approach,
serverless architecture, allows for maximum transparency whilst maintaining security, so that
both parties know only the information they need to. It has web and mobile ends. 
From mobile, customers can keep track of the purchased product quality. 
From web, companies(producers) can keep track of their products.
